# EcoForge Daily Standup & Quick Operations Checklist

**Version**: 1.6 – March 22, 2026

**Purpose**: Fast, 5–10 minute daily pulse for 1 operator to confirm system stability, catch early drifts, and log essentials. Designed as a "standup" ritual — scan, tweak, record, move on. Foundation for weekly trends and monthly reviews. Scalable from Container 1 solo op to multi-node hubs.

### Daily Quick Scan (5–10 minutes, ideally morning)
- **Water Quality Snapshot** (record values)  
  - Temp: 78–86°F (target 82°F)  
  - DO: >5 mg/L (aim >6)  
  - pH: 6.8–7.5  
  - Ammonia: <0.5 ppm (alert if >0.3)  
  - Nitrite: <1 ppm  

- **Feeding & Biomass Check**  
  - Feed tilapia: Adjust to 1–3% estimated body weight (use last weigh-in proxy)  
  - Duckweed harvest: Thin 20–50% if mat >70% coverage (keep 1–2 cm thick)  
  - Worm tea: Check brew (ready if EC 0.5–2.0 mS/cm; dilute if >2.0)  

- **Visual & Health Pulse** ✅  
  - Fish: Active swimming, no flashing/gasping/clustering  
  - Plants: Leaves vibrant, no wilting/yellowing  
  - Biofloc: Coffee-brown, no foul odor or floating scum  
  - System: No leaks, pumps humming, no unusual noise/vibration  

- **Energy & Support Systems**  
  - Solar charge: Battery SOC >80% (or trending up)  
  - Geothermal buffer: Temp stable (no alarms)  
  - Biogas: Pressure gauge check (steady, no drop)  

- **Quick Actions / Logging** (1–2 min)  
  - Apply any immediate tweaks (e.g., add air if DO low, shade if algae hint)  
  - Log snapshot to sheet/app (values + notes + photos if anomaly)  
  - Note any flag for weekly review (e.g., "ammonia creeping → watch carbon")  

### Daily Troubleshooting Flags ⚠️ (Act same day)
- Ammonia >0.5 ppm → Increase carbon (duckweed/molasses), reduce feed 20%  
- DO <5 mg/L → Add/check air stones, clean filters  
- pH <6.8 or >7.5 → Buffer (dolomite for low, vinegar/carbon for high)  
- Fish stress signs → Immediate aeration boost + visual root cause hunt  
- Duckweed crash → Thin hard, check light/nutrients  

### Success Thresholds (Daily Average/End-of-Day)
- All water params in range (no red flags)  
- Fish/plants visually healthy  
- Energy systems stable (SOC trending positive)  
- Log entry completed (data trail intact)  
- No emergency interventions needed
