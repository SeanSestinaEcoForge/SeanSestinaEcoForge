**Version**: 1.1 – March 22, 2026

**Thesis Statement**

A modular, hybrid regenerative system integrating Nile × Blue hybrid tilapia aquaponics, Eisenia fetida vermiponics, multi-strain biofloc (Bacillus subtilis/licheniformis + Nitrosomonas europaea/Nitrobacter winogradskyi + Rhodopseudomonas palustris), Lemna minor duckweed cultivation, biogas-derived CO₂ enrichment, and geothermal/micro-hydro/solar energy achieves **60–85% practical loop closure** in water, nutrient, and oxygen cycles under real-world off-grid conditions and short-duration Mars-analog stressors (0.3–0.6 atm, elevated CO₂, regolith analogs), simultaneously delivering sovereign food-energy-water security for off-grid homesteads operated by 1–2 people and generating verifiable bioregenerative life support data to contribute to long-term human Mars settlement readiness.
