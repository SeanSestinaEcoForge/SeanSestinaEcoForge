# EcoForge Revenue & Sales Playbook

**Version**: 1.0 – March 22, 2026

**Purpose**: Practical guide to turn true excess production into steady cash flow while keeping 80–90% for internal abundance. Designed for Container 1 and scalable to the Premium Hub. Focus on low-effort, realistic channels first.

### 1. Revenue Philosophy
- Self-consumption always first (80–90% of output stays internal).  
- Only sell true excess.  
- Tiered approach: Local/direct → Wholesale → High-value B2B/corporate.  
- Conservative 2026 wholesale pricing used (no retail markup assumed).

### 3. Sales Channels & Tactics
**Tier 1 – Local/Direct** (Months 1–12)  
- Farmers markets (weekly booth)  
- Direct to restaurants (samples + consistent small batches)  
- Online: Etsy, Facebook Marketplace, local groups (worms, castings, biochar)  
- CSAs and co-ops (greens + tilapia shares)  

**Tier 2 – Wholesale & Bulk** (Months 6+)  
- Garden centers, bait shops, regional distributors  
- Online bulk platforms  

**Tier 3 – Premium B2B & Corporate** (Months 12+)  
- Tesla/SpaceX ecosystem (ECLSS kits, regenerative data)  
- Research institutions and grants  

### 4. Pricing & Packaging Guidelines
- Start at lower end of range to build relationships.  
- Bundle products (greens + tilapia, castings + worms starter kit).  
- Premium markup: 20–30% for “EcoForge Certified” regenerative branding.  
- Track monthly: excess → sales → adjust quarterly.
